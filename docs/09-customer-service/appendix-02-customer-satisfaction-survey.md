# Appendix 2 — Customer Satisfaction Survey

**Parent procedure:** [09 – Customer Service](./)

## Purpose

To collect structured feedback from customers on the quality of the laboratory's services. Results are compiled and analyzed by the Quality Manager and reported during the management review.

## Instructions

1. The **Quality Manager** distributes the survey to active customers at the defined interval (at least annually).
2. Surveys may be sent by email, online form, or on paper — whichever format the customer prefers.
3. Responses are compiled into the summary table (Part B) for trend analysis.
4. Actions arising from feedback are tracked to completion.
