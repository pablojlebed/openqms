# Appendix 2 — List of External Documents

**Parent procedure:** [00 – Document and Record Control](./)

## Purpose

To identify and track all documents of external origin that the laboratory relies on for its quality management system and technical activities.

## Instructions

The Quality Manager reviews this list at least annually (or when notified of a new edition) to verify that the laboratory holds the current version of each external document. When an updated version is identified, affected internal procedures must be reviewed for impact.
