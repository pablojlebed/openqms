# Appendix 4 — Registry of Records for Retention / Central Archive

**Parent procedure:** [00 – Document and Record Control](./)

## Purpose

To maintain a traceable log of records sent to the central archive and records disposed of after their retention period has expired.

## Instructions

When records are transferred to the central archive or disposed of, the responsible person records the action in this registry. The Quality Manager approves all disposals.
