# Complaint, Nonconformity and Corrective Action Procedure

**Procedure number:** 14  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clauses 7.9, 7.10, 8.7

## Purpose

To define the process for receiving and resolving complaints, identifying and controlling nonconforming work, and implementing corrective actions to eliminate root causes.
