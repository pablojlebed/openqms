# Appendix 2 — List of Approved Suppliers of Products and Services

**Parent procedure:** [06 – Externally Provided Products and Services](./)

## Purpose

To maintain a single, current register of all suppliers that have been evaluated and approved to provide critical products or services to the laboratory. Only suppliers listed here with a status of "Approved" or "Conditionally approved" may be used for critical items.

## Instructions

1. The **Quality Manager** updates this list whenever a supplier evaluation is completed or a supplier's status changes.
2. Before placing an order for a critical product or service, **purchasing personnel** verify that the supplier appears on this list with an active approval status.
3. Expired approvals must be flagged and the supplier re-evaluated before further use.
4. Never delete a supplier from this list. When a supplier is no longer approved, change its status to "Withdrawn" and record the date and reason. The historical record supports traceability.
