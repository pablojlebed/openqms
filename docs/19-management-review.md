# Management Review Procedure

**Procedure number:** 19  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 8.9

## Purpose

To define the frequency, inputs, outputs, and follow-up actions for management reviews to ensure the continuing suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the QMS.
