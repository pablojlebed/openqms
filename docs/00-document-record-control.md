# Document and Record Control Procedure

**Procedure number:** 00  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 8.4

## Purpose

To define the controls needed for the identification, creation, review, approval, distribution, access, retrieval, retention, and disposal of documents and records within the laboratory's quality management system.

## Scope

Applies to all QMS documents (procedures, policies, work instructions, forms) and all records generated as evidence of conformity and effective operation.
