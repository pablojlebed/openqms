# Internal Audit Procedure

**Procedure number:** 18  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 8.8

## Purpose

To plan and conduct systematic internal audits to verify that the QMS conforms to the laboratory's own requirements and to ISO/IEC 17025, and that it is effectively implemented and maintained.
