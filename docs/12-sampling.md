# Sampling Procedure

**Procedure number:** 12  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 7.3

## Purpose

To define the methodology for sampling plans, sample collection, transport, and handling when the laboratory performs sampling as part of its testing or calibration activities.
