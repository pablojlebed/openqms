# Continuous Improvement Procedure

**Procedure number:** 21  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 8.6

## Purpose

To establish a systematic approach for identifying, evaluating, and implementing improvements to the laboratory's QMS, processes, and technical activities.
