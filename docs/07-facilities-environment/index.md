# Facilities and Environmental Condition Procedure

**Procedure number:** 07  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 6.3

## Purpose

To ensure that laboratory facilities and environmental conditions do not invalidate results or adversely affect the required quality of measurements.
