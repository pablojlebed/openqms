# Equipment Maintenance, Calibration and Verification Procedure

**Procedure number:** 08  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 6.4

## Purpose

To ensure that all equipment used in laboratory activities is properly maintained, calibrated, and verified to be fit for its intended purpose.
