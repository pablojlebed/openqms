# Appendix 1 — Equipment Register

**Parent procedure:** [08 – Equipment Maintenance, Calibration and Verification](./)

## Purpose

To maintain a complete and current inventory of all equipment subject to the laboratory's calibration, verification, and maintenance program. The register provides a single point of reference for equipment identification, status, and location.

## Instructions

1. The **Quality Manager** adds each item when it is commissioned and updates the register when status changes occur (calibration, repair, relocation, decommissioning).
2. The **Technical Manager** reviews the register at least annually to confirm accuracy and completeness.
3. This register is a controlled document managed per [Procedure 00](../00-document-record-control/index.md).
