# Appendix 1 — Sampling Plan and Record

**Parent procedure:** [12 – Sampling](./)

## Purpose

To document the sampling plan before collection and to record the details of the sampling event as it occurs. This record ensures traceability from the test result back to the specific sampling conditions.

## Instructions

1. The **Technical Manager** completes or approves Part A (Sampling Plan) before sampling begins. For routine sampling covered by a standard SOP, enter the SOP reference instead of completing Part A in full.
2. **Sampling personnel** complete Part B (Sampling Record) at the time of collection — not retrospectively.
3. Part C (Chain of Custody) is completed when custody tracking is required by the customer or regulatory framework.
4. The completed record is filed per [Procedure 00 — Document and Record Control](../00-document-record-control/index.md).
