# Quality Assurance Procedure

**Procedure number:** 11  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 7.7

## Purpose

To define quality assurance activities — including proficiency testing, interlaboratory comparisons, and internal QC — used to monitor the ongoing validity of results.
