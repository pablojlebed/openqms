# Addressing Risks and Opportunities Procedure

**Procedure number:** 05  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 8.5

## Purpose

To establish a systematic approach for identifying risks and opportunities that could affect the laboratory's ability to achieve its objectives and deliver valid results.
