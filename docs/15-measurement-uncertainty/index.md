# Evaluation of Measurement Uncertainty Procedure

**Procedure number:** 15  
**ISO/IEC 17025 reference:** Clause 7.6

## Purpose

To define the approach for identifying all uncertainty contributions, evaluating measurement uncertainty, and reporting it appropriately in test and calibration results.
